BNPL Experience — Increasing Conversion in High-Stakes Fintech

Company: Ingosstrakh
Role: Product Designer (End-to-End)
Timeline: 2022–2024

Outcome

  • +18% checkout conversion (A/B tested, 50k+ sessions)
  • -25% drop-off at payment step
  • +30% improvement in user comprehension (usability testing)

Validated after 2-week A/B test on live traffic.

Why This Matters

Insurance is a high-friction product: users make decisions under uncertainty, with financial risk and low trust. BNPL shifts perception from “expensive commitment” to “manageable decision”.

Problem

>40% drop-off at payment stage due to high upfront cost and unclear terms.

  • No clear mental model for installments
  • Lack of transparency
  • Fear of hidden conditions

Constraints

  • Strict financial regulations
  • Backend limitations (approval logic)
  • Multiple edge cases

My Role

  • Owned full design lifecycle
  • Defined UX strategy
  • Designed flows + UI + logic

User Flow

Product → BNPL Entry → Plan Selection → Terms → Approval → Payment

Solution

  • Pre-calculated plans (reduces decision fatigue)
  • Monthly-first communication
  • Inline explanation of terms
  • Robust edge-case handling

Why This Works (Design Taste)

  • Removes need for user calculations
  • Aligns with how people think about money (monthly, not total)
  • Reduces perceived risk through transparency
  • Minimizes cognitive load at each step

The difference wasn't visual — it was how information is structured and revealed.

Impact & Validation

  • +18% conversion uplift
  • -25% drop-off reduction
  • Higher trust in user interviews

Key Insight

Users don't evaluate UI — they evaluate risk. Good fintech design reduces uncertainty, not just friction.

Relevance to Revolut
