Luma One

The interface disappears. Space becomes intelligent.

PROMPT:\nUltra realistic Apple-style render of Luma One placed in a modern interior. A human is present in the scene, slightly out of focus, interacting naturally with the environment. Luma emits soft ambient light that spreads across walls and furniture, subtly transforming the mood of the space. The light should feel alive and adaptive, with soft gradients and reflections on surfaces. The product is centered, premium materials like matte glass and aluminum.\nBACKGROUND: minimal modern apartment, warm neutral tones\nRADIUS: 2xl

Halo System

Halo is the operating system for physical space. It connects devices, energy and interaction into one seamless layer.

PROMPT:\nApple-style product render of Halo device on a table. A human hand is placing a smartphone onto the surface for wireless charging. At the same time, Luma One is connected to Halo and charging as part of the same system. Multiple devices are visible in a minimal composition, suggesting "works with phone and home ecosystem" without logos. Soft glowing light indicates energy transfer. Clean premium materials, soft shadows.\nBACKGROUND: minimal desk setup\nRADIUS: xl
PROMPT:\nMinimal Apple-style display panel near Luma One. The screen shows simple UI with lighting controls, environment modes and emotional expressions. A user hand interacts with the display.
